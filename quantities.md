## 有专门名称的 SI 导出量 (22个)

BIPM SI Brochure Table 2。每个导出量由基本量通过物理关系定义。

| 导出量 | 专门单位 | 符号 | 基本单位表示 | 关系式 |
|--------|---------|------|-------------|--------|
| plane angle | radian | rad | 1 | $\alpha = s/r$ |
| solid angle | steradian | sr | 1 | $\Omega = A/r^2$ |
| frequency | hertz | Hz | s⁻¹ | $f = 1/T$ |
| force | newton | N | kg·m·s⁻² | $F = ma$ |
| pressure, stress | pascal | Pa | kg·m⁻¹·s⁻² | $p = F/A$ |
| energy, work, heat | joule | J | kg·m²·s⁻² | $E = F\cdot r$ |
| power, radiant flux | watt | W | kg·m²·s⁻³ | $P = E/t$ |
| electric charge | coulomb | C | A·s | $q = \int I\,dt$ |
| electric potential | volt | V | kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻¹ | $V = P/I$ |
| capacitance | farad | F | kg⁻¹·m⁻²·s⁴·A² | $C = q/V$ |
| electric resistance | ohm | Ω | kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻² | $R = V/I$ |
| electric conductance | siemens | S | kg⁻¹·m⁻²·s³·A² | $G = 1/R$ |
| magnetic flux | weber | Wb | kg·m²·s⁻²·A⁻¹ | $\Phi = \int B\,dA$ |
| magnetic flux density | tesla | T | kg·s⁻²·A⁻¹ | $B = \Phi/A$ |
| inductance | henry | H | kg·m²·s⁻²·A⁻² | $L = \Phi/I$ |
| Celsius temperature | degree Celsius | °C | K | $t = T - T_0$ |
| luminous flux | lumen | lm | cd·sr | $\Phi_v = I_v\Omega$ |
| illuminance | lux | lx | cd·sr·m⁻² | $E_v = \Phi_v/A$ |
| activity (radionuclide) | becquerel | Bq | s⁻¹ | $A = -dN/dt$ |
| absorbed dose | gray | Gy | m²·s⁻² | $D = E/m$ |
| dose equivalent | sievert | Sv | m²·s⁻² | — |
| catalytic activity | katal | kat | mol·s⁻¹ | — |

## 量纲系统

BIPM SI Brochure 2.3.3。任意量 $Q$ 的量纲：

$$\dim Q = \text{L}^\alpha\text{M}^\beta\text{T}^\gamma\text{I}^\delta\Theta^\epsilon\text{N}^\eta\text{J}^\nu$$

指数由物理方程确定。
